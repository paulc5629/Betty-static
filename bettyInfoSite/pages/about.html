Project Betty

A diary application for people with Diabetes

Idea

Using my experience as a diabetic I always thought how different activities could affect my blood sugar level.

With this lurking in my mind I create a app where I can upload all my information that can affect my levels.

Data

Take charge of your data. Stop letting other companies tell you who and what can share you data.

With us you can allow medical professionals and sponsors access your data for specific amounts of time.

Learn

Ever wondered how stress and exercise affect your sugar levels?

With us you can see how it affects your glucose levels by studying automatically generated charts
